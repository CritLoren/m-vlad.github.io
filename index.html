Features

  • Extensions

    extension

    Online reading from well-known sources such as KissManga, MangaDex, and many more.

  • Sync

    sync

    Track your library across multiple serices like MyAnimeList, Anilist or Kitsu.

  • Customization

    format_paint

    Configurable reader with multiple vieweing modes, reading directions and other settings. such as library organization via categories and light and dark themes.

  • Automated Updates

    get_app

    Update your library on a schedule and get the newest chapters on your device as soon as they're online.
